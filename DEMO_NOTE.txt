WHAT TO EXPLORE IN THE LIVE DASHBOARD:

1. KPI Cards (top of page)
   - 412 t/day waste generated across 5 pilot Tier-2/3 Karnataka cities
   - 17 active illegal dumping hotspots
   - 9 crop-residue burning alerts (7-day)
   - Estimated ₹8.4L/month savings from AI-recommended route optimization

2. Waste Generation Trend & Forecast (chart)
   - 6 months of actual data
   - 2-month AI-generated forecast (dashed line)

3. Pilot City & Alert Status (right panel)
   - Live status per city: Hubli-Dharwad, Belagavi, Davangere, Ballari, Tumakuru
   - Color-coded: normal / warning / alert

4. AI Decision Assistant (bottom panel) — THE CORE FEATURE
   - Click any suggested question chip, or type your own:
     "Which wards have overflowing bins today?"
     "What is the waste trend in Hubli-Dharwad?"
     "Any crop burning alerts this week?"
     "Where should we build a compost plant?"
   - Returns a natural-language, action-oriented answer
   - In production this queries Vertex AI (Gemini) + BigQuery in real time
     across IoT sensors, satellite imagery, and citizen reports

PROBLEM SOLVED:
Bengaluru absorbs nearly all waste-tech attention and funding in Karnataka.
Tier-2/3 cities and their surrounding agricultural belts have almost no
AI-driven decision support for solid or agricultural waste — despite rapid
urban growth and heavy crop-residue burning. ClearCycle AI closes that gap.

TECH STACK (Production):
Google Cloud — Vertex AI (Gemini API), BigQuery, Cloud Storage, Pub/Sub,
Cloud Run, Firebase.
